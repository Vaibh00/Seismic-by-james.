Seismic — Encrypted blockchain for private DeFi & apps

Seismic integrates base-layer encryption with secure hardware to protect state across transitions. The stack is open-source and developer-friendly, with components like seismic-revm and seismic-client. (Source: Seismic docs & blog.)
